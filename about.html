Banana Bread Origins

Banana bread started getting popular in the 1930's when baking soda and powder started
growing in popularity. The recipe appeared in both a Pillsbury and Chiquita recipe book,
furthering it's position as a baking staple.

Why Banana Bread?

Banana bread is super easy! Almost everyone has some bananas that are at
that too ripe stage that go uneaten. Banana bread uses only the most common
cupboard staples that makes this a super fun and simple recipe that everyone
will enjoy.
