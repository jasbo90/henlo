Ingredients

  • 3 mashed bananas (4 for extra moist bread)
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 1 cup sugar (and a few pinches of sugar to top the batter once in the pan)
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 1/2 cups of flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla

Directions

Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees

Cream together butter and sugar (use hand mixer on room temperature butter to mix with sugar until sugar crystals are super fine and consistency is soft and creamy)

Add beaten eggs and crushed bananas

Combine well

Sift flour, baking soda, and salt into the mixture

Add vanilla

Mix until just combined, don't over mix

Pour into greased pan or parchment lined pan loaf pan

Bake at 350 degrees for 55 mins

Take out of pan once finished to let cool on cooling rack for 10 mins

Enjoy!
